CEO MESSAGE

Welcome to Shahid Engineering Works online portal! These are exciting yet challenging times at Shahid Engineering and I hope our website gives an opportunity to our valued visitors around the world to learn more about our leading-edge operations, quality performance, strategies, services, and professional values being adopted to serve the industry. This platform is a part of the external manifestation of our commitment to transparency and open communications with all our stakeholders as well as a large number of our patrons interested in our activities.

Automotive sector is one of the most dynamic engineering fields in this era of cutting edge technology. Since the company’s inception in 1978, Shahid Engineering Works has played a pioneering role in the development of automotive parts for leading OEM’s in Pakistan. Our company works tirelessly to serve clients with a passion and commitment of quality integration.

Good governance, transparency and leadership are the very core of company’s corporate values which are reflected in every aspect of day-to-day operations. The company prioritizes its responsibility towards the industries it serves with an intrinsic motivation of maintaining high standards of quality.

Shahid Engineering is a success story that would not have been a reality without the contribution of our valued customers and dedicated employees who have shared our incredible journey thus far. We appreciate the ongoing support received from those who are interested in Company’s activities and pledge to continue with our efforts to exceed expectations. We look forward to serving them and other stakeholders as per our legacy in the future.

I look forward to detailing all these and other developments at the appropriate time, and our website will remain the ideal source of updated information. Thank you for visiting Shahid Engineering Works website.

Syed Shahid Ahmed
