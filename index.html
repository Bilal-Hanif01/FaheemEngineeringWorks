PRESS SHOP

Our press shop is equipped with English, German Japanese Presses of varies capacities and types. which is core strength of our Production facility. Actually press shop is Identity of Shahid Engineering because of Sheet metal Manufacturer.

WELDING SHOP

Our welding shop reinforces our production facility as assembly section of our press shop part. Almost all parts pass through this process. Our welding shop is equipped with all three most common welding type of equipment including Electric arc welding, Co welding and spot welding.

PAINT SHOP

Our 80% products are Painted finished product directly assemble on assembly line of OEM’s. Previously our paint production was vendorized but by the grace of Almighty Allah Shahid Engineering has recently achieved mile stone of developing paint shop facility. our paint shop is equipped with Powder coating facility as well as spray paint facility.

TOOL SHOP

Die making starts and Die maintenance complete the cycle of sheet metal manufacturing. Shahid engineering has Precision die making facility in tool & Die section.
